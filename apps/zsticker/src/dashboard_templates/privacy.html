Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

zSticker only accesses your Google Drive to upload AI-generated LINE stickers for your own use. We do not collect, share, or sell your personal data. The only data processed is the image files generated by the application.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your Google Drive access is used strictly for storing images temporarily so they can be pushed to your LINE Official Account or LINE group via the LINE Messaging API. We do not read or modify any other files in your Google Drive.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Google APIs for Google Drive storage and Google Sheets integration. We also use the LINE Messaging API. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information.

4. Data Retention

Images uploaded to your Google Drive belong to you and remain there until you delete them. Our application does not store any copies of your data on our servers beyond temporary processing.

# 🎨 The Ultimate LINE Sticker Prompt Guide
*Based on official LINE Creator Market Guidelines*

To create a successful LINE Sticker set, the images must be highly expressive, clearly visible on both light and dark backgrounds (usually achieved with a white border), and easily readable in a small format.

---

## 🌟 1. Core Principles of a Perfect LINE Sticker Prompt

When generating images for LINE stickers using DALL-E 3, Midjourney, or Pollinations.ai, always include these **Core Modifiers** in your prompt:

- **Style:** `flat vector illustration, clean lines, solid colors, kawaii style, minimalist design, cute chibi character`
- **Background:** `isolated on solid white background` *(This makes background removal 100x easier)*
- **Visibility (LINE Guideline):** `thick white stroke around the character` *(Helps the sticker pop on dark chat backgrounds)*
- **Emotion:** Must be exaggerated and instantly recognizable.
- **Framing:** `full body` or `waist up, centered`

## 🛠️ 3. How to create "Message Stickers" (Blank space for text)

LINE "Message Stickers" allow users to type their own text inside a bubble or board held by the character.

> **Prompt:** A cute chibi panda character holding up a completely blank, empty white whiteboard. The panda is peeking over the top of the whiteboard. Flat vector illustration, kawaii style, clean outlines, isolated on a solid white background, thick white border around the panda and the board, no text on the board.

## 📝 4. Checklist for Submission (Official LINE Rules)

Before uploading to LINE Creators Market, ensure your generated images meet these criteria:
1. **Remove the Background:** Use a tool (like remove.bg or Python's `rembg`) to make the background 100% transparent.
2. **Size:** Maximum 370px width x 320px height (must have even numbers for dimensions).
3. **Margin:** Leave a margin of about 10px around the image so it doesn't touch the edge of the screen.
4. **Format:** PNG format only.
5. **No Prohibited Content:** No alcohol, violence, copyright infringement, or overly complex photographs (stick to illustrations).

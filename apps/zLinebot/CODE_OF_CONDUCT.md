# Code of Conduct (EN)

## Our Pledge
We are committed to providing a welcoming, harassment-free experience for everyone regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity/expression, experience level, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity/orientation.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent; discuss ideas, not people.
- Give and receive feedback professionally.
- Focus on project goals and user safety.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or doxxing.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeated disruptive behavior after warnings.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove comments, close issues/PRs, or ban accounts for violations.

## Reporting
Report incidents to project maintainers through a private channel (email or direct message used by repository maintainers).
